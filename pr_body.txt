**What**
Implemented the `Notification` organism, a transient application-level feedback component, along with full BDD scenarios and vitrine documentation.

**Why**
The system lacked a standardized way to deliver application-level feedback that doesn't interrupt the user flow. As established in the `epic-1a2b-form-ergonomics` roadmap, we need robust feedback components integrated deeply with our design tokens and doctrine.

**Decisions made**
- Implemented `Notification` without nesting curves, relying on a structural geometric left border (`var(--accent)`) to indicate the notification's intent, staying true to the Curva & Concreto aesthetic.
- The component correctly supports dark mode by leveraging the existing global `--soft` variants and gracefully inverting them.
- Implemented correct `role` (`alert` vs `status`) and `aria-live` regions based on the intent (`danger`/`warning` vs `info`/`tip`).
