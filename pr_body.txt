What: Implement native `DatePicker` and `TimePicker` form controls.
Why: The system needed semantic date and time inputs that adhere to the Curva & Concreto visual language and integrate correctly with the `FormField` composition pattern for accessibility.
Roadmap Items Advanced: `task-5e6f-date-time-pickers` (parent: `tactical-3c4d-native-inputs`).
Decisions: Kept strictly to native HTML5 inputs (`<input type="date">` / `<input type="time">`) to avoid heavy third-party dependencies, guaranteeing excellent accessibility out of the box while skinning them to our strict token definitions. Svelte context injection is used to inherit properties from `FormField`.
Trade-offs: Native pickers look different across browsers, but this tradeoff is acceptable compared to the bundle bloat of building custom, accessible calendar widgets from scratch.
Test evidence: Added full Vitest coverage for `DatePicker` and `TimePicker`, confirming correct rendering, size class application, and `FormField` context inheritance.
