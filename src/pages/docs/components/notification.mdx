# Notification

The Notification component is an organism that provides asynchronous system feedback. While Tooltips provide immediate, contextual information attached to a trigger, Notifications are application-level messages that appear independently of the user's current focus.

## Doctrine

Notifications adhere to the Curva & Concreto aesthetic by utilizing `--soft` background colors paired with a structural `--accent` border strip. Instead of nesting curves inside curves, the colored border strip provides structural contrast and anchors the message.

In Dark Mode, the `--soft` backgrounds invert to maintain geometric contrast, while the vibrant `--accent` strip is preserved for semantic clarity.

### Accessibility

Notifications automatically apply correct ARIA roles and live regions based on their `intent`:
- `info` and `tip` use `role="status"` and `aria-live="polite"`.
- `warning` and `danger` use `role="alert"` and `aria-live="assertive"`.

**Timing Adjustable (WCAG 2.2.1)**: Notifications can auto-dismiss via the `timeout` prop. To ensure users have enough time to read the message, the timeout is automatically paused when the user hovers over or focuses the notification, and resumes when interaction ends.

## Examples

### Intents

The `intent` prop defines the semantic meaning and visual style of the notification.

<div class="not-content" style="display: flex; flex-direction: column; gap: 1rem; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
  <Notification intent="info" title="Update Available" message="A new version of the system is ready to be installed." dismissible={false} client:load />
  <Notification intent="tip" title="Report Generated" message="Your monthly summary has been exported successfully." dismissible={false} client:load />
  <Notification intent="warning" title="Connection Lost" message="We're having trouble connecting to the server. Retrying..." dismissible={false} client:load />
  <Notification intent="danger" title="Payment Failed" message="Your card was declined. Please check your billing details." dismissible={false} client:load />
</div>

<Notification

### Dismissible

By default, notifications are dismissible. You can disable this by setting `dismissible={false}`.

<div class="not-content" style="display: flex; flex-direction: column; gap: 1rem; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
  <Notification intent="info" title="Dismissible Notification" message="Click the close button to dismiss me." client:load />
  <Notification intent="info" title="Non-dismissible Notification" message="I cannot be closed manually." dismissible={false} client:load />
</div>

```svelte
<Notification
  intent="info"
  title="Dismissible Notification"
  message="Click the close button to dismiss me."
/>

<Notification
  intent="info"
  title="Non-dismissible Notification"
  message="I cannot be closed manually."
  dismissible={false}
/>
```

### Auto-dismissing (Timing Adjustable)

Provide a `timeout` in milliseconds to automatically dismiss the notification. The timer pauses on hover or focus, complying with WCAG 2.2.1.

<div class="not-content" style="display: flex; flex-direction: column; gap: 1rem; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
  <Notification intent="tip" title="Auto-dismissing" message="I will disappear after 5 seconds. Hover over me to pause the timer." timeout={5000} client:load />
</div>

```svelte
<Notification
  intent="tip"
  title="Auto-dismissing"
  message="I will disappear after 5 seconds. Hover over me to pause the timer."
  timeout={5000}
/>
```
