jsdom natively triggers the `open` state change on `<details>` when clicking `<summary>` even if we do an `event.preventDefault()` inside an `onclick` handler. To properly test `disabled` preventing toggle in Vitest/jsdom, we must intercept it at the `ontoggle` level and force `details.open = false`.
